Pan Seared Steak

Description

For this recipe I'm going to tell you how to make a delicious pan seared steak indoors. It is easier than you think and is how I personally made one of the best steaks of my life.

Ingredients

  • Steak of any cut
  • Salted Butter: 3ish tbs
  • Garlic: maybe 4ish cloves

Steps

  1. First you're gonna want to get a pan and preheat it on high (preferably a cast iron) also preheat your oven to 350-400f.
  2. Put that steak on there after putting some pepper on it and sear both sides maybe 1-2 mins per side and 1ish mins on the edges if it is thicker.
  3. Once you get the steak seared put it in the oven for 5mins (more if you want your steak more done).
  4. Take the steaks out of the oven and put butter and garlic(and spices if you want) in one side of the pan, opposite of the pan.
  5. Take a spoon and start basting the steak with the butter and garlic to finish it off.
  6. Alakazam some steak ma'am.
