Pasta Noodles

Description

In this recipe you are going to be making pasta noodles from scratch. The main problem is rolling out the dough to cut, but we'll get through it.

Ingredients

  • flour: 1 cup
  • eggs: 1 full plus 1 yolk

Steps

  1. First make a cirlce of flour on a table
  2. Then make a hole in the flour in the center and add your eggs.
  3. Slowly mix egg and flour until the dough becomes one conformed unit.
  4. Roll out your dough as thin as you can, if you have a pasta roller it makes this much easier, but it isn't necessary.
  5. Cut your dough and then boil in water with some salt.
  6. Alakazam some pasta ma'am.
