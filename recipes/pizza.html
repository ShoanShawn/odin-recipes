Pizza

Description

Making pizza is one of the simplest and most complex things you can do, Certain aspects of it is very controllable and others are moreso semi-random. It is important to remove any sort of outside factor so for the recipe I would recommend having fresh ingredients, imparticular to the cheese, yeast, and sauce. The other ingredients freshness are definitely still important, but a much lesser degree. Without futher ado, let's get that dough.

Ingredients

  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Sugar: 1 tbs
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Yeast: 1 tsp to 2 tsp (depending on rise time)
  • Olive Oil: 1tbs to 2 tbs (preference)
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings
  1. First thing to do is get your yeast going with your water and sugar
  2. Next you need to mix it with your other dough ingredients (the ones that have amounts listed) besides olive oil, unless you plan to knead your dough by hand.
  3. Start kneading the dough (preferably in a mixer) maybe for about 5 minutes. This is the finnicky part, you might need to add more flour or water, when you have about 2 mins left add your oil.
